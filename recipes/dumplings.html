Pork Dumplings

Description

Dumplings are a food that is associated with Chinese cuisine and is usually a light dough that wraps around a filling. In this recipe, the dumplings will be a Chinese style dumpling with pork filling that can be steamed or friend.

Ingredients

Dipping Sauce

  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped Chinese chives
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon chile-garlic sauce(such as Sriracha®)

Dumplings

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped Chinese chives
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 ½ tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 50 dumpling wrappers
  • ½ cup vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. Combine dipping sauce ingredients to make the dipping sauce
  2. Mix pork, garlic, egg, chives, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger until thoroughly combined
  3. Make dumpling by putting filling in dumpling wrapper and sealing with water
  4. Heat 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    Place 8 to 10 dumplings in the pan and cook until browned, about 2 minutes
    per side. Pour in 1 cup of water: cover and cook until the dumplings are
    tender and pork is cooked through, about 5 minutes
  5. Repeat for remaining dumplings and enjoy!
