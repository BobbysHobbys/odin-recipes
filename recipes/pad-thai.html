Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is a stir fry Thai dish that involves noodles, protein, vegetables, and a flavor-packed sauce with peanuts, bean sprouts, and lime that can be added.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup distilled white vinegar
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind pulp
  • 1 (12 ounce) package dried rice noodles
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu, cut into 1/2 inch strips
  • 1 ½ tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ½ cups ground peanuts
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground, dried oriental radish
  • ½ cup chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 cups fresh bean sprouts
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

    • Steps

    1. To prepare Pad Thai sauce: In a medium saucepan over medium heat, blend sugar,
      vinegar, soy sauce and tamarind pulp.
    2. To make Pad Thai: Soak rice noodles in cold water until soft; drain. In a large
      skillet or wok over medium heat, warm oil and add garlic and eggs; scramble the
      eggs. Add tofu and stir until well mixed; add noodles and stir until cooked.
    3. Stir in Pad Thai sauce, 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt. Stir
      in peanuts and ground radish. Remove from heat and add chives and paprika.
    4. Serve with lime and bean sprouts on the side.
